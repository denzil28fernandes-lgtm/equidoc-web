Standard Employment Agreement

This Employment Agreement (the "Agreement") is made and entered into as of this day by and between the Employer and the Employee.

1. Position and Duties

The Employer agrees to employ the Employee as a full-time General Laborer. The Employee agrees to perform all duties assigned by the Site Supervisor. The Employee shall work a minimum of fifty (50) hours per calendar week, from Monday through Saturday. Overtime compensation is explicitly waived under this Agreement.

2. Compensation

In consideration of the services rendered, the Employer agrees to pay the Employee a flat monthly salary of $1,400.00 USD (One Thousand Four Hundred Dollars), payable on the last business day of each month. No additional benefits, health insurance, or paid time off shall be provided.

3. Early Termination & Penalties

The Employee commits to a minimum employment term of twenty-four (24) consecutive months. If the Employee resigns, abandons their post, or is terminated with cause prior to the completion of this twenty-four (24) month period, the Employee shall be held strictly liable for a "Recruitment and Relocation Reimbursement Fee" of $3,500.00 USD. This fee shall be immediately deducted from any final paycheck, and the Employer reserves the right to pursue further legal action to recover the remaining balance.

4. Non-Compete

Upon termination of this Agreement for any reason, the Employee agrees not to accept employment with any competing firm within a 100-mile radius for a period of three (3) years.
